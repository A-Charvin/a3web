Data Lead

Coming from a higher education in statistics/mathematics or general engineering, you have a confirmed experience of at least 5 years as a data analyst with ideally a first experience in management. You master advanced statistical methods and you have strong analytical and synthetic skills, you know how to transform them into relevant recommendations.

  • Knowledge of Data Science algorithms such as social graph or machine learning is a plus.
  • You have a perfect command of SQL, a data visualization tool (ideally Tableau) and Data Engineering concepts (ETL, Kafka, etc.) in a cloud environment.
  • Knowledge of the Redshift database and another language or development environment such as Python, R, Scala or Spark is a plus.
  • Endowed with strong interpersonal skills, you are ready to discover the challenges related to Ankama's multiple professions: marketing, gis-design, production, finance, customer relations, community management...
  • A good knowledge of the world of cartography and more particularly of GIS would be highly appreciated.
  • Fluency in English is desired.

IT DEPARTMENT 1:Scale Lindsay

System and IT security engineer

Within the Information Systems Department, as a system and security engineer, you will participate in the identification and anticipation of risks related to infrastructure and information security. You will also ensure the application of security and prevention processes, as well as their updates. In the context of complex environments, you also have the task of making this same information system available and functional at all times.

  • Design, implement and monitor the IT systems security strategy.
  • Apply and industrialize the deployment of security updates
  • Analyze and verify the compliance of systems and equipment
  • Carry out curative maintenance of obsolete or defective elements.
  • Establish reports and propose improvements to the systems in place
  • Perform IT security audits
  • Produce security dashboards
  • Dealing with security incidents
  • Monitor security action plans
  • Analyze incidents that have occurred, related to non-compliance risks, and apply remedial measures
  • Provide reporting (recipe, minutes, activity report, etc.)
  • Update technical documentation and perform IT and security monitoring
  • Educate staff on the importance of computer security
