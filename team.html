Meet the faces behind the scales

 

Chief technical lead & Creative Head - Anand Charvin G

Talks about #AI #Technology #Enterprise DBMS #Animation&VFX

As a key member of leadership team Charvin leads (financial decision-making, staff growth and development, safety and labor compliance and employee benefit offerings).He continues to provide innovative and advanced technical solutions to meet its clients' needs. His organizational and Technical skills and his passion for spatial analysis have contributed to the success as the go-to company in the GIS Consulting field.  He is interested in leveraging AWS cloud computing solutions, ESRI JavaScript API, ArcGIS web app builder, and mobile GIS applications to meet the needs of clients.   Considered as a geek, Charvin has developed his own GIS tools to provide tailor-made solutions in different projects, thus enhancing client’s experience and autonomy.

Cartography Team Lead- Suruchi Tolia

Talks about #Cartography #Datavisualization #Spatial Analysis

She is a highly talented individual with years of proven experience in supervision, coordination and maintenance of standards and adherence to quality control of cartographic illustrations for various technical reports; performs spatial analyses of archeological data and creates spatial analytical graphics for analysts using geographic information systems and other digital methods. She is passionate about making maps and geographic analysis simpler, more beautiful, and more enjoyable than ever. Using a variety of tools, from motion graphics and interactive maps to remote sensing and 3D modeling, she builds the visual bridges that connect complex science and public understanding. 

Research & development - Jose Augustine

Talk about #Remote sensing #Earth Observation #Machine learning

Surveying and CAD lead - Rajeev Pathak
